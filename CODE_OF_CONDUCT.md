# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Krishiv a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or employer.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, technically honest, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while accepting correction.
- Respect privacy and coordinated security disclosure.
- Avoid discriminatory, threatening, sexual, or personally abusive behavior.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository
owner's private contact channel. Maintainers may warn, moderate, temporarily
restrict, or permanently remove participants based on severity and pattern.
Maintainers must avoid conflicts of interest and preserve reporter privacy where
possible.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
